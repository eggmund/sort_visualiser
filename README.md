### Controls:
#### Sorts:
**Key** | **Sort**
--- | ---
**1** | Bubble Sort.
**2** | Selection Sort.
**3** | Quick Sort.
**4** | Shell Sort.
**5** | Comb Sort (very similar to shell sort).
**6** | Radix LSD Sort (Base 10).

#### Array functions:
**Key** | **Sort**
--- | ---
**S** | Shuffle.
**R** | Reset array.
**I** | Invert/reverse array.
**Q** | Cancel current sort.

#### Display modes:
**Key** | **Sort**
--- | ---
**C** | Circle.
**B** | Bars.
**D** | Dots.
**P** | Pixels.
